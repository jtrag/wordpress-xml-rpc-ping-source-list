The best and most recent xml rpc ping sources list. Created July of 2015 & Updated Frequently.

  • Click/Open the "List.txt" file.
  • Select the URLs/Text within the "Lists.txt" file, and copy it to your clipboard (Highlight the text, right click on it, and click "Copy").
  • Go to your Wordpress Admin Panel, and click on Settings>Writing
  • In the XML-RPC Ping Source List Box at the bottom of the Settings>Writing page, paste (Right click in the box, and click "Paste") the text you copied from the "List.txt".
  • Save the changes in the Wordpress Admin Panel, and you're all set!
